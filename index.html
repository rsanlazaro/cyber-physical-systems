Dynamic Exo-Systems for Humans

This area is centered in the development of projects focused on healthcare with the aim of developing external rehabilitation systems that people or health professionals can use to improve life quality of the population. The projects developed within the scope of this department include differential neural networks of finite element for the study of deformation of soft objects, and physiological signals classification for interaction in virtual reality systems for the control of robotic manipulators and mobile robots.
